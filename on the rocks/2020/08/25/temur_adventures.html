On the Rocks

There's No 'I' in Temur Adventures(Standard)

Julian Leal

25 Aug 2020 - 5 min read


Welcome back one and all to the fifth installment of On the Rocks! After multiple big shake-ups in Standard, I can sort of play base Simic decks with a reasonable conscience. So today I reunited with one of my favorite decks from the beginning of this year: Temur Adventures!

After the disappointing performance of Naya Adventures last time, this deck really brings the heat. Once again, I cast Bonecrusher Giant a lot. Take a drink every time I say, “Crush,” it’ll be fun.
