The Year of Brew

Week 19: All Permanents, All the Time

Julian Leal

23 May 2021 - 5 min read


And we’re back with the Arc of All or Nothing, doing another ALL deck with All Permanents. Instants? Sorceries? Never F@%#king heard of them. This deck is stupid, enjoy.

Deck 19: Bant



Yes, yes, yes. We’re playing Chulane, Teller of Tales , but we have a good reason. For any self respecting all permanents deck, you gotta go for the Primal Surge and Laboratory Maniac combo. Slam the surge, flip your deck over and rejoice in a flurry of triggers. And that’s sort of what we’re doing…

…except this is an actual all permanents deck. NO Primal Surge . So to slam jam through our deck we have a million and one creatures who either ramp us or draw a card on entry. Chulane also does both of these key effects for us when we cast any of our 55 creatures.

The only real spicy things about this deck are 1) it plays Tangleroot , heavily underrated and great for chaining, chaining, chaining creatures like we want. 2) its a hella budget deck. Sub $100 and easily could get to below $50 with a couple cuts. 3) there is a secret other win condition using Epic Struggle . Good luck.
